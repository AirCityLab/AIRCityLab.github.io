Seeking MS/PhD student

We are seeking outstanding students for MS/PhD Graduate Assistantship positions in Urban Science and Urban Resilience. The students will work on several projects on disaster science and urban science with a group of collaborators from urban planning, civil engineering, computer science, geography, and landscape architecture.

Applicants are expected to have experience in urban analytics on one or more of the following methodological fronts: machine learning, natural language processing, computer vision, urban simulation, or spatial modelling.

In February 2022, the Carnegie Classification of Institutions of Higher Education designated UTSA as an R1 institution, placing the university among the top 4% of research universities in the United States.

Please contact Dr. Wei Zhai at wei.zhai@utsa.edu for more information.
